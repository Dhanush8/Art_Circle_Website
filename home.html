EVENTS

"Thath Nada Wahi" is the biggest musical extravaganza organized by Art Circle FOS UOP and undergraduates of faculty of Science perform in this show.Last "Thath Nada Wahi" show was held in 2018 at University Gymnasium and this show organize one time for a 4 years."Thath Nada Wahi" next music extravaganza will held again near future...

"Kalyani" is a music review programme organized by Us.We invite a special guest to explain the meaning of songs and Our members perform in this event.Anyone who love old meaningful songs can join with this event and feel the music.We organize this event anually and last "Kalyani" programme was held in 2019 at Faculty common and next programme will held on 18th January 2023.

"Faculty Padura" is the most important music event organized by Us.Not only music but also dancing and drama events are also there.Everyone enjoy with this event and all the undergraduates in the faculty help to make success this event.We organize this event anually and last "Faculty Padura" programme was held in 2019.We will organize our next programme near future...

"Faculty Padura" is not the one and only Padura in Faculty of Science.There are many other paduru events organized by our departments."CHEMSOC Padura", "ES Padura" and "Maths Padura" are some of them.CHEMSOC Padura is organized by Chemistry Department, ES Padura is organized by ES Department and Maths Padura is organized by Maths Department.Likewise other departments also organize these paduru events and we perform there.All the music lovers do not miss these paduru events and everyone enjoy with these paduru programmes.Next Padura will held near future...
